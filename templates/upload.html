Required CSV Format:

Your CSV must contain these columns:

  • city - City name
  • state - State/province
  • country - Country
  • week_label - Week identifier (e.g., "Wk 40")
  • cases - Number of cases

What happens next?

  1. CSV data is validated and processed
  2. City is created/updated in the system
  3. Epidemiological indicators are calculated
  4. You're redirected to the city dashboard
