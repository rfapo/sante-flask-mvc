Santé – Intelligent Epidemiological Surveillance

Santé is a solution to monitor and anticipate epidemiological outbreaks at the municipal level. The platform integrates clinical data, digital signals (e.g., symptom searches), environmental and demographic data to compose a nowcasting and short-term forecast, supporting managers in decision making.

  • Data ingestion via CSV upload by city.
  • Key indicators such as R(t), R0 and hospitalization rate.
  • Interactive dashboard inspired by the provided prototype.

How to Use

  1. Login.
  2. Go to Upload CSV and send a file with columns: city,state,country,week_label,cases.
  3. After processing, you will be redirected to the city dashboard.

This project is an educational MVP; in production, adjust ingestion, validation and predictive models.
